Success Rate Overview

India's sports success rate has significantly improved across various disciplines, showcasing remarkable achievements on the global stage. Cricket leads with a success rate of 90-95%, driven by dominant performances in ICC tournaments and players like Virat Kohli. Athletics has flourished with stars like Neeraj Chopra and Hima Das, achieving an 85-95% success rate in key events. Badminton players P.V. Sindhu and Saina Nehwal have consistently won medals, contributing to a 90-92% rate.
